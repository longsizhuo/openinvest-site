Privacy

Last updated: 2026-06-24

TL;DR. OpenInvest is self-hosted and local-first. The author runs no server and receives none of your data. Your portfolio, ledger, config, and committee transcripts stay on the machine where you run it. LLM and market-data calls go directly from your instance to the providers you configure, under your own keys and their terms. No telemetry, no analytics, no tracking.

What we collect

Nothing. OpenInvest has no backend operated by the author and no account system. There is no server that receives your portfolio, holdings, trades, or queries.

Where your data lives

  • Your holdings, cash ledger (a local SQLite file), configuration, and committee transcripts are stored on your own machine or self-hosted instance.
  • The internal ledger is a record-keeping database only — it connects to no real payment processor or brokerage.

Third-party calls made by your instance

  • LLM providers: running the committee sends prompts to the model provider you choose — Anthropic (via Claude Code, Coordinator path) or a provider such as DeepSeek (Direct path) — using your own API key and subject to that provider's privacy policy. OpenInvest does not proxy, store, or log these centrally.
  • Market data: prices are fetched from public sources (e.g. Yahoo Finance via yfinance) directly by your instance.
  • Optional publishing: if you enable the optional P&L / accuracy features, only the aggregated, redacted data you choose to publish is pushed to your own GitHub repository. No symbol-level holdings are published.

This website

This page (openinvest.involutionhell.com) is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. It sets no cookies and runs no analytics or trackers.

Not investment advice

OpenInvest is an LLM-driven decision-support tool. It does not place trades and does not constitute investment advice. Decisions are yours.
